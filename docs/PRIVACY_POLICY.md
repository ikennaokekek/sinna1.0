# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Sinna respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1) What We Collect
**Account & Billing**
- Email, company name (if provided)
- Subscription and billing info (processed by Stripe)

**Operational Data**
- API request metadata (timestamps, method, paths, headers like user agent)
- Job metadata (IDs, presets, durations, status)
- Usage metrics (jobs, minutes, storage, rate‑limit counters)

**Content & Artifacts**
- Video URLs you submit for processing
- Generated artifacts (captions, audio descriptions, color analysis, transformed videos)
- Stored securely in Cloudflare R2 via signed URLs

**Diagnostics**
- Error logs and performance metrics (e.g., via Sentry, Prometheus)

## 2) How We Use Data
- Provide and operate the Service (process content, generate artifacts)
- Authenticate requests and enforce usage limits
- Improve reliability, performance, and security
- Send operational emails (e.g., API key delivery, incident notices)
- Process payments and manage subscriptions via Stripe

## 3) Sharing
We do not sell personal data. We share data only with subprocessors essential to the Service:
- **Stripe** (payments and billing)
- **Cloudflare R2** (artifact storage & signed URLs)
- **AssemblyAI / OpenAI** (media processing, where configured)
- **Render** (hosting)
- **Sentry / Prometheus** (monitoring and diagnostics)

We may disclose data to comply with law, protect rights, prevent fraud/abuse, or as part of a merger/acquisition.

## 4) Security
- TLS for data in transit
- Signed URLs for artifact access
- Access control for API keys and internal services
- Least‑privilege access for staff and systems

## 5) Retention
- Account and billing data: while your account is active and as required by law
- Usage and logs: typically 30–365 days depending on purpose
- Artifacts: retained per plan and your configuration, then expired/deleted

## 6) Your Rights
Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data.
- Contact: privacy@sinna.site
- We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable law

## 7) International Transfers
We may process data in multiple regions via our providers. We use appropriate safeguards for transfers where required.

## 8) Children’s Privacy
The Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 9) Changes
We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or the dashboard.

## 10) Contact
- Privacy: privacy@sinna.site
- DPO (if applicable): dpo@sinna.site
- Website: https://sinna.site

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
